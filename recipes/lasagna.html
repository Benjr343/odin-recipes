Lasagna Recipe

A scrumptious Pasta dinner, perfect for the weekday

The preferred meal of Charlie Kelly, from "It's Always Sunny in Philidelphia".

Only the finest minced beef should be used, and is delicious when served immediately.

Ingredients

  • Beef Mince
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Mozzarella
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Recipe instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over a low heat. Fry the onion until softened
  2. Add the mince and fry on a medium heat until golden.
  3. Parboil the pasta sheets 10 minutes before the mince and onion mixture is cooked.
  4. Assemble Lasagna in large baking/roasting Tray
  5. Cook for 5 minutes
